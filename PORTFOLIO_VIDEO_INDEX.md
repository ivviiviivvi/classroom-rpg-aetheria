### Quick Troubleshooting

**Problem**: "This feels overwhelming"  
**Solution**: Start with just the 90-second version. Get that done, then expand if needed.

**Problem**: "I don't have video editing skills"  
**Solution**: Use Canva Video (easiest) or hire an editor from Fiverr ($200-500).

**Problem**: "I'm not comfortable on camera"  
**Solution**: Use avatar_mode="none" and focus on voiceover with strong visuals.

**Problem**: "This is taking too long"  
**Solution**: Follow the "Path B: Hybrid" approachâ€”outsource the parts you're stuck on.
